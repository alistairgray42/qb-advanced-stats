About

If you're not familiar with what quizbowl is, welcome! This might be a good place to start.

Instructions for using scoresheets created by this site can be found on the Southern California Quizbowl website.

This site was created and is maintained by Alistair Gray, to whom you should direct all requests for access, questions, etc. The scoresheets were created by Dana Lansigan and revised by Jonathan Luck, who also wrote much of the backend code. Versions of them have been used at most UCSD and Southern California tournaments for years.
